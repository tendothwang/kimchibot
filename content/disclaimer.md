## Trading Risk Warning

Cryptocurrency trading, especially with leverage, is extremely risky.

- You can lose more than your initial deposit.
- Leveraged positions can be liquidated in seconds.
- Past performance does not predict future results.
- Backtests routinely overstate real-world performance.
- Automated bots can malfunction and cause unexpected losses.
- Exchanges can fail, freeze withdrawals, or go insolvent.

**Never trade money you cannot afford to lose completely.**

If you're considering trading crypto, consult a licensed financial advisor in your jurisdiction first.
