## Get in Touch

I welcome questions, feedback, corrections, and stories from other bot builders.

### The Best Ways to Reach Me

**X (Twitter):** [@kimchibot_x](https://x.com/kimchibot_x)
The fastest way. I post bot updates there and reply to DMs and mentions.

**GitHub:** [tendothwang](https://github.com/tendothwang)
Open an issue on any of my public repositories if you found a bug in code I've published or want to discuss technical details.

### What I'm Happy to Hear About

- **Mistakes in my posts** — If something is technically wrong or a number doesn't add up, please tell me. I'd rather fix it than have it stay wrong.
- **Your own bot experiences** — What worked, what didn't, what surprised you.
- **Specific questions** — "How did you handle X?" questions get the best answers. General "can you teach me to build a bot?" questions are harder to help with.

### What I Can't Help With

- **Financial advice.** I don't give it. Nothing on this site is a recommendation to trade.
- **Managing your money.** I don't run accounts for others.
- **Trading signals or tips.** The bot trades for me; I don't sell its signals.
- **"Guaranteed profit" schemes.** These don't exist. If you're looking for one, this isn't the site.

### Response Time

I'm a one-person operation. I try to respond within a week, but sometimes it takes longer. Patience appreciated.

### Business Inquiries

For collaboration proposals or business-related questions, please reach out via X DM and mention it's a business inquiry.
